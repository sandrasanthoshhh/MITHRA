Women Empowerment Government Schemes

1. Beti Bachao Beti Padhao Yojana

The Beti Bachao Beti Padhao scheme focuses on the welfare of the girl child and aims to ensure the survival, protection, and education of girls across India.

This scheme provides financial assistance to pregnant and lactating women for their first live birth. It ensures improved health and nutrition for mothers and children.
